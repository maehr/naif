NAIF
National Approach for Interoperable repositories
and Findable research results


NAIF is a project initiated and funded by eight Swiss Higher Education Institutions and co-funded by swissuniversities. The project aims to enable more interoperability and standardization across Swiss institutional repositories. The project has started in July 2025 and will end in December 2026.
