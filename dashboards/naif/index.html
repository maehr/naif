This dashboard was prepared by Dr Moritz Mähr for NAIF. It brings together 57 Swiss higher education institutions and shows how many are DORA signatories, CoARA signatories, or CoARA members.

Included institution groups are universities, university institutes, universities of applied sciences (UAS), UAS institutes, and teacher education institutions. Hover over a marker to see the full institution name, then click for more detail.

On the CoARA map, a black centre dot marks CoARA members. The current dataset includes 25 DORA signatories, 16 CoARA signatories, and 12 CoARA members.

The source data used in this dashboard is available from the download tab.
