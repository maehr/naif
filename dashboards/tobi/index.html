This dashboard presents data from the TOBI project (Towards Open Bibliometric Indicators) on the findability and publication output of 55 Swiss Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) in five bibliometric databases: Dimensions, Scopus, Web of Science, OpenAIRE, and OpenAlex.

The TOBI project, conducted at ETH Library, investigated how well Swiss HEIs are represented in commercial and open bibliometric databases. Its findings are particularly relevant for research assessment and the development of open bibliometric indicators.
