DARIAH Annual Event 2026

Related Topic
In-person DARIAH Annual Event in Rome (26-29 May 2026) on infrastructures of engagement in digital arts and humanities.
Organizer

DARIAH ERIC

Published

May 26, 2026

Details

  • Date: 26-29 May 2026
  • Location: Universita degli Studi Roma Tre - Dipartimento di Studi Umanistici, Via Ostiense 234, Rome, Italy
  • Registration: https://annualevent.dariah.eu/registration/
  • Call for papers: https://annualevent.dariah.eu/call-for-papers/
  • Event page: https://annualevent.dariah.eu/

About

The DARIAH Annual Event 2026 focuses on “Digital Arts and Humanities With and For Society: Building Infrastructures of Engagement”. The event brings together researchers and practitioners from arts, humanities, cultural heritage, and information sciences to exchange work on digitally enabled research and collaboration.

The programme includes DARIAH internal meetings on 26 May and the main conference from 27 to 29 May.
